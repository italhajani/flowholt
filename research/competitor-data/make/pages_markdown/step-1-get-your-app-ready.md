# Step 1. Get your app ready

1 min

In the [first part of this guide](/create-your-first-scenario)﻿, you set up Slack and Google apps to create a basic notification system. Now, to expand your scenario and send targeted mobile notifications to the Sales Manager about any US-based prospects received, you need to set up the Make mobile app.

The Make mobile app brings your automations to your smartphone, sending you push notifications about important events. This helps team members receive real-time alerts without checking Slack or email. For Sales Managers on the go, these instant notifications ensure they never miss high-priority prospects.

To set up the Make mobile app:

1

Go to your device's app store:

* ﻿[iOS App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/integromat/id1178593383 "iOS App Store")﻿

* ﻿[Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.integromat.app "Google Play Store")﻿

2

Search for **Make** or **Make (formerly Integromat)**.

3

Download and install the Make app.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/yjwhINLS3fc-NXg38fV_d-9aQgVBH38WUAI32e_6QdF-20250314-133521.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

4

Log in to Make. In the left side sidebar, click **More > Devices**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/6gzj-G_GbyLefdk6ukoth-20250930-111841.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

5

Click **+ Add device** in the top right corner.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/nm5O-TVjZ3F6Zj7TZCAr1-20250930-111912.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

6

In the **Add device** window, type a name for your device (for example, "My sales phone") and click **Continue.**

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/bRnMwoM0vaBorqoG8Nngf-20250930-111948.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

The screen with the QR code will appear.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/y0swH1efNVRCt0FcmeFB6-20250930-112021.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

7

Go back to your mobile phone and open the **Make** app. In the screen that appears, tap **Scan a QR Code**. Scan the code you see in Make. If scanning doesn't work, you can manually enter the code displayed.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/yjwhINLS3fc-NXg38fV_d-ZHw9ll4sR51JSDR-bcXu_-20250330-181039.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/yjwhINLS3fc-NXg38fV_d-gXGS4U3f71cxpVIdz_UMJ-20250330-181550.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

8

Come back to Make and click **Save.**

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/2KywVE4K_Xr45JyI7DsHS-20250930-112214.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

You should see the confirmation message that the device has been added successfully, and see the device you've added on the **Devices** page.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/W6WK9hL6atkPSw77kOyw5-20250930-112316.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

9

In the Make mobile app, go to **Settings > Notifications**, and switch on the **Allow notifications** toggle. This will allow you to be notified whenever a new prospect is added to your spreadsheet.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/yjwhINLS3fc-NXg38fV_d-whqLkJy0eeUONt_NCn0q_-20250330-181715.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

﻿

Your Make mobile app is installed and set up in Make. You are ready for the next step — adding a router to direct your scenario so that all prospects go to Slack, while only US-based prospects trigger mobile notifications.
