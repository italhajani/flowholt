# Two-factor authentication

5 min

You can make your Make﻿ account more secure using two-factor authentication (2FA). 2FA means that there are two different factors (your Make﻿credentials and an additional verification code) needed to authenticate you as a user when logging into your Make﻿ account.

You must have an authenticator app installed on your mobile device.

## Enable two-factor authentication

There are many authenticator apps available (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator, or 1Password). We will use the **Google Authenticator** app in our example.

1

Click your profile icon in the upper-right corner and select **Profile**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Os7Sr564DOb8RM8xfqY04-20251002-104555.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

2

Click the **2FA** tab for two-factor authentication, and then the **Enable** button.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/yQI7-66r_8Q18I5pWizST-20251002-105230.png "Document image")

﻿

3

In the **Enable Two-Factor Authentication** dialog box, you will see a QR code or a key in case you can't scan the code.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/RMkUfvkPPSghCrJDIyN9H-20251002-105500.png "Document image")

﻿

4

Install and open the **Google Authenticator** app on your mobile device ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.authenticator2 "Android"), [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/google-authenticator/id388497605 "iOS")).

5

In the **Google Authenticator** app, click **Scan a QR code** and scan the QR code you got in step 3.

6

Enter the six-digit **verification code** generated by the **Google Authenticator** app and your Make﻿ password in the **Enable Two-Factor Authentication** dialog box, and click **Enable**.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/QnTYZ5-FNB6PVWtWCt60v-20251002-110251.png "Document image")

﻿

7

Click the **Download codes** button to download the TXT file containing all your one-time passwords.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Y0ifIv3dIfo0lc-vayHyj-20251002-110728.png "Document image")

﻿

Save the provided one-time passwordsin a safe place (not on your mobile device). You can use these one-time passwords to recover access to your account if your mobile device has been stolen or lost, or if the authenticator mobile app has been removed from your phone.

## Sign in using two-factor authentication

After signing out, you can now log in using 2FA.

1

Go to the Make﻿ login page.

2

Enter your Make﻿ credentials and click Sign In (or sign in using Google, Facebook, or GitHub).

3

Open the authenticator app (e.g. Google Authenticator) on your mobile device to get the verification code.

4

Enter the verification code provided by the authenticator mobile app in the **2FA code** field, and click **Sign in**.

If you have lost your mobile device or accidentally deleted the authenticator app, you can use one of the one-time passwords you store after enabling the 2FA on your Make account (step 6 in the [How to enable two-factor authentication](/two-factor-authentication#FonfX)﻿ section above).

## Disable two-factor authentication

1

Log in to your Make﻿ account.

2

Click your profile icon in the upper-right corner and select **Profile**.

3

Click the **2FA** tab for two-factor authentication, and then the **Disable** button.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/CMGf7kM28fYobGoPXLYHN-20251002-111004.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

4

Enter the verification code generated by your authenticator app on your mobile device, enter your Make﻿ password, and click **Disable**.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/LHxDQT0-FabFrii3CutWI-20251002-111105.png "Document image")

﻿

Two-factor authentication is now disabled on your account.

## Switch to another authenticator app or change your mobile device

If you want to use another app for the two-factor authentication or another phone, you first need to disable the two-factor authentication on your Make account using your current authenticator app or one-time password.

1

﻿[Disable the two-factor authentication](/two-factor-authentication#fciuj)﻿ on your Make﻿ account.

2

Install another authenticator app on your mobile device (or the same app on the new mobile device).

3

﻿[Enable the two-factor authentication](/two-factor-authentication#FonfX)﻿ on your Make﻿ account using your newly installed authenticator app.
