# Scenario inputs scheduling, OpenAI modules support GPT-5

4 min

### Scheduling update for required scenario inputs

Previously, it was unclear in the product that scenarios with required [scenario inputs](https://help.make.com/scenario-inputs-and-outputs "scenario inputs") must have on-demand scheduling. Now, when you try to save a scenario with required scenario inputs, you will be prompted to update the scenario scheduling to on demand and save the scenario again.
