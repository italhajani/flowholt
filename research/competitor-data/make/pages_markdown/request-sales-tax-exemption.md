# Request sales tax exemption

0 min

If you are an organization exempt from sales tax, [contact us](https://www.make.com/en/ticket "contact us") using the following details:

* **Topic:** Account, Billing & Affiliate

* **Category:** Billing

* **Topic details:** Tax exempt

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/yjwhINLS3fc-NXg38fV_d-0x-uZA6uIh_R5AS3x2s1y-20250724-092624.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

Once you reach out, we'll send you instructions on submitting your tax-exempt documentation. After we review and verify your documentation, you'll receive an email confirming your tax-exempt status has been approved.
