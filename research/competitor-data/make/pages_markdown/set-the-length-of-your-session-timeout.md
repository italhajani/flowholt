# Set the length of your session timeout

1 min

For security purposes, you may wish to control the length of the session timeout.

You can choose to set your session timeout from 15 minutes to 24 hours. **This option is only available to Organization administrators and owners.**

1

Open your **Organization** dashboard in the left sidebar.

2

In the upper-right corner, click the three dots next to the **Organization settings**, select **Set session timeout**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/ARrE1ZBPusi3eYdf7AU5N-20251003-132535.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

3

Select how long users can be inactive before they are automatically logged out and click **Save**.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/8R06Shud7m23x6usOdBfR-20251003-132706.png "Document image")

﻿

Your session timeout is saved.

If a user belongs to multiple organizations, the shortest session logout time applies.
