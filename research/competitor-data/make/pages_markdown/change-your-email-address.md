# Change your email address

1 min

You can change your email address from your **Profile** page.

1

Click your profile icon in the upper-right corner and select **Profile**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/fIHnvkHBdg6pitYmfvk0x-20251002-072443.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

2

In the upper-right corner of the **Profile page**, click the three dots next to the **Profile settings**, and select **Change email**.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Oioqvdq5mTdieGUxTeWNF-20251002-073106.png "Document image")

﻿

3

Enter your new email address in the **New email address** field. Enter your **Current password**, and click **Save**.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/QAEcsMmPYFr6GDCQWbW-R-20251002-073719.png "Document image")

﻿

4

A confirmation email will be sent to your **current email address.** Click the **Change email address** button in the email to approve the change.

![Change your email step 5](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/yAufeXqD1oGWOPBNi5MAm-DGICwT_q_If-d3k8Ekn7q-20241203-131127.png "Change your email step 5")

﻿

5

You'll be redirected to the login page and asked to check your new email for a verification link.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/WWGq8fnQeXv5b0XG-jH91-20251002-074653.jpg "Document image")

﻿

6

Check the new email you introduced in Step 3 for a verification email. Open it and click the **Verify new email** button.

7

You'll be redirected to the login page. Your new email will be displayed in the **Email** field, and you will see a message indicating your email address has been successfully changed.

![Change your email step 6](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/yAufeXqD1oGWOPBNi5MAm-ndncmsw59cSHOgFzurM1V-20241203-131208.png "Change your email step 6")

﻿

8

You'll also receive a confirmation email, sent to the new email address associated with your account.

![Change your email step 7](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/yAufeXqD1oGWOPBNi5MAm-9CCzzhJ2gS6DpzZmreGQg-20241203-131237.png "Change your email step 7")

﻿

For security purposes, verification of your change email request must be completed within 24 hours, at which point the links will expire.

If you can't access your email or login to make these changes, please contact our support team. You'll be asked to provide additional information about your account so we can make the change on your behalf.
