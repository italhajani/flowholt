# Change payment details

8 min

You can change your payment information at any time.

﻿Make﻿ accepts the following payment methods:

* Credit/debit card

* Digital wallets: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Amazon Pay, and Cash App Pay

* Link (for one-click checkout)

* Klarna (to pay in installments or via custom financing)

* ACH direct debit (US bank accounts): Valid for monthly and annual subscriptions only

For more information on payment options, see the [FAQs](/change-payment-details#faqs)﻿ below.

Available methods may vary based on your region.

## Enter payment details

1

In the left sidebar, click **Org**.

2

Under **My Plan**, click **Subscription**.

3

Click **Update payment method**, or **Add payment method** if you're on a Free plan.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Rt3pwNxd1qyCIRDVxujgp-20251010-110557.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

4

Under **Select new payment method type**, select your new payment type.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/i7r0BspZmT2L_-9oUJwu0-20260123-074617.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

Available methods may vary based on your region.

5

For the **Set as default** fields, select **Yes** if you want to use the card as your default payment method for subscriptions and extra credits.

6

Click **OK**.

7

In the checkout screen, enter the required information.

8

Click **Save**.

Upon completing the instructions, a notification confirming the change appears in the lower-right corner.

## Change your billing address

If you're an organization owner or admin, you can change your billing address in Make﻿ at any time.

1

In the left sidebar, click **Org**.

2

Click the three dots next to **Organization settings**, and select **Change billing details** from the dropdown menu.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/TfpKwgyjgs6xJv3XE9eCl-20251010-112220.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

3

Enter your information in the allocated spaces (email, name, address).

If you have a company account, select **Yes** under **I am a business**.

If you want Make﻿ to issue the invoice to your company (and not to you personally), make sure to add your company name and Company Tax ID.

4

Click **Save**.

The new billing information is automatically reflected in your next invoice.

Only customers in the US pay sales tax. We calculate sales tax based on your zip code.

## Change your billing email

By default, Make﻿ sends invoices for payments to the email address the organization owner provided when creating the organization.

If you're an organization owner or admin, you can change your billing email in Make﻿ at any time.

To change your billing email:

1

In the left sidebar, click **Org**.

2

Click the three dots next to **Organization settings**, and select **Change billing details** from the dropdown menu.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/TfpKwgyjgs6xJv3XE9eCl-20251010-112220.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

3

In the dialog, update the **Email** field with your new billing email, and update other fields if needed.

4

Click **Save**.

Your new billing email is automatically taken into account for your next invoice.

## FAQs

**Which payment methods are available in my country?**

Available methods vary by region. You'll see all options automatically at checkout, including Klarna and specific digital wallets, based on your location.

**Can I switch my current subscription to one of these new methods?**

Yes! You can update your payment information at any time. See the [Enter payment details section](/change-payment-details#enter-payment-details)﻿.

**Is there an extra fee for using Klarna or digital wallets?**

No, Make does not charge additional transaction fees for these payment methods. However, please check Klarna's terms for any interest that may apply to specific financing plans.

**Are these methods available for all plan types?**

Yes, new payment methods are available for Core, Pro, and Teams plans. For Enterprise customers, please contact your Account Manager for invoicing options
