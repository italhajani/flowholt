# Product Use Cases

## WooCommerce SEO Content Writer

Target experience for FlowHolt:

- User describes a recurring business task in plain language.
- Chat thinks through the task and explains the proposed flow in user-friendly language.
- The platform chooses or suggests specialist steps such as keyword research, product writing, and meta optimization.
- Inputs are made explicit, for example `product_name` and `product_category`.
- The result is a ready workflow draft that can be edited in Studio and then run.

Example use case:

- A store owner uploads 4 to 5 WooCommerce products daily.
- FlowHolt should be able to produce SEO-optimized product descriptions, meta titles, meta descriptions, and image alt text suggestions.
- The workflow should feel like a helpful content team assembled from known tools and agent blocks, not a vague black box.
