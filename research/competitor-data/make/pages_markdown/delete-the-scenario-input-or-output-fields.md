# Delete the scenario input or output fields

1 min

To delete the scenario inputs or outputs:

1

In the Scenario Builder, click the scenario﻿ inputs and outputs icon.

2

Find the item you want to delete.

3

Click **X** next to the item you want to delete.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/x7lkSQuYX4ZNDpjo81Hnu-20260121-143741.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

4

Click **Save**.

The deleted item will be removed from the scenario﻿ inputs and outputs dialog box and from your scenario.
