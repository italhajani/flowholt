# Step 3. Add your first app

1 min

At this step, you'll add the first application and module that will trigger your scenario.

A trigger module is what kicks off your scenario, for example, when somebody fills in a form, when a new email arrives in your inbox, or when a row is added to a spreadsheet.

In this guide, we're using "Watch New Rows" for Google Sheets as our trigger.

1

﻿[Sign in](https://www.make.com/en/register "Sign in") to Make﻿. You'll see the main dashboard, where you can access all platform features.

2

Click **+ Create a new scenario** in the top-right corner to open the Scenario Builder.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Ky0XPIA0SfqfHVTjY634N-20250929-130811.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

You can also find the **+ Create a new scenario** button in the **Scenarios** tab located in the left sidebar.

3

In the top-left corner, click **New scenario** to update your scenario's name.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/3n3U11QjVZAPrObjXou6r-20251103-192455.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

4

Enter **New** **prospect** **notification** (or any name you prefer). Click the **Save** icon on the Scenario Builder toolbar to save your scenario.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/EgD7W-c0b8249wneKcRN2-20251103-192531.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

5

Click the giant plus in the center of your canvas. This will open a window with the list of apps that you can add to your scenario.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/mE_upp3SXiEjXguCpY5je-20251103-192611.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

6

Search for **Google** **Sheets** in the search bar or browse the available apps.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/V7dG80LF0Hk40p9o_WiRl-20251103-192639.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

If you select the wrong app, click **Back** to return to the list.

7

Select **Google** **Sheets** and then choose **Watch** **New** **Rows** from the available modules.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/ea04i_Xb_eCuBLYBLEH8x-20251103-192710.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

Now you've added your trigger module that will start your scenario whenever new prospects are added to your spreadsheet. Before you can configure it, you need to create a connection to Google Sheets.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/1AI8_KKUbFa6D7jHOyZW3-20251103-192844.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿
