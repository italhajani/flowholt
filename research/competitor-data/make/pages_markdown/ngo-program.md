# NGO program

1 min

The [Make for NGO program](https://www.make.com/en/ngo-program "Make for NGO program") is designed to help NGOs maximize their impact by automating processes and streamlining operations. We engage with NGOs from all over the world, particularly in the areas of childhood & education, health & hunger, and the earth & environment.

As part of the program, Make﻿ provides selected NGOs with a license for 12 months, with the opportunity to renew after the initial period.

Additionally, we choose a handful of NGOs every year to participate in our high-engagement program where they get the chance to work with our employees to learn how to use Make﻿ to its maximum potential.

﻿[Learn more](https://www.make.com/en/ngo-program "Learn more") about the benefits of the Make for NGO program, eligibility criteria, and the application process. We look forward to hearing your story.
