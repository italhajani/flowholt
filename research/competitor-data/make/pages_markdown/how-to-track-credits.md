# How to track credits

9 min

Track your [credit usage](/credits)﻿ to understand which activities consume the most credits and adjust as needed.

Several places in Make﻿ offer a different view of your credit usage. The following outlines where you can track credit usage and the information available.

| **View** | **Credit usage** | **Best for** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Dashboard** | * Average daily credit usage  * Credits left | Overview and trends |
| **Subscription** | * Credits in plan  * Total credits used | Billing |
| **Credit usage** | Credits used per run | Analyzing run-level usage in all scenarios﻿ and AI agents |
| **Scenarios** | Credits used per scenario﻿ | Identifying high-usage scenarios﻿ |
| **Scenario diagram** | Credits used in a scenario﻿  ﻿ | Analyzing a scenario's usage in detail |
| **Scenario Builder** | Credits used per module | Analyzing module-level usage and debugging |
| **Scenario history** | Credits per individual run | Analyzing run-level usage in a scenario |

In the next sections, learn more about where you can track credit usage in Make﻿.

## Dashboard

**Dashboard** tracks total and daily credit usage for the current billing period. To find it, go to **Org** in the left sidebar.

At the top, you can view average daily usage, remaining credits, and the date when your credits reset.

In **Usage**, you can view your credit usage trend in the current billing period. Hover over **Credits** to view the credits and extra credits in your plan.

## Subscription

**Subscription** tracks total credits purchased and used in the current billing period. To find it, go to **Org** in the left sidebar, then click **Subscription** under **My Plan**.

In **Credits**, you can view the credits used from the total. You can also buy extra credits or enable extra credits auto-purchasing.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/mjbbehI7uDoAQLPGz00tW-20251121-081008.png "Document image")

## Credit usage

**Credit usage** tracks your credit usage per run across all scenarios and AI agents. To find it, go to **Org** in the left sidebar, then click **Credit Usage** under **My Plan**.

This table includes the following information:

* **Name:** Name of the scenario or AI agent that used credits

* **Type:** Type of feature that used credits, whether a scenario﻿ or an AI agent

* **Credits:** Number of credits used in the run

* **Data transfer:** Amount of data transferred in the run

* **Usage recorded:** The date and time of the credit usage

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/6qph_luSG_VmpFL-FWMvC-20251121-102817.png "Document image")

﻿

In the **Name** column, click an item to go to the scenario﻿ history of a specific run and view its details. **Name** items are only clickable when the **Type** is scenario, as AI agent history doesn't exist.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/JHo2fgMLxOjktTSVASuQs-20251121-124809.png "Document image")

﻿

Your pricing plan determines the number of days that credit usage entries are stored. See **Execution log storage (days)** on [Make's pricing page](https://www.make.com/en/pricing "Make's pricing page").

## Credit usage notification

As your organization approaches its credit usage limit, the organization owner and admins will receive an email detailing this information.

* At 75% usage, the organization owner and admins will receive an email if your consumption is high in comparison to the amount of time that has elapsed in the month. For example:

* If you use 75% of your credits and only 40% of the month has elapsed, you will get a notification email.

* If you use 75% of your credits and 75% or more of the month has elapsed, you won't get a notification email.

* At 90% usage, the organization owner and admins will always receive an email.

* At 100% usage, the organization owner and admins will always receive an email.

## Scenarios

The scenarios﻿ list tracks credit usage by scenario﻿. To find it, go to **Scenarios** in the left sidebar.

The credit icon next to each scenario﻿ indicates the number of credits used in a scenario﻿ over the current billing period.

## Scenario diagram

The scenario diagram tracks total credit usage in a scenario. To find it:

1. Go to **Scenarios** in the left sidebar.

2. Open a scenario﻿ in the scenario﻿ list.

In **Scenario usage**, you can view the total credits used in a scenario. **History** lists individual scenario﻿ runs and their credit usage. Click any run to view it in more detail.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/FfdtHEso97xVBZrm6hRcE-20251121-101044.png "Document image")

﻿

## Scenario Builder

The Scenario﻿ Builder tracks credit usage per module run. To find it:

1. Go to **Scenarios** in the left sidebar.

2. Open a scenario﻿ in the scenario﻿ list.

3. Click the canvas.

When you click **Run once** or **Run this module only**, you can view credit usage for the run in the output bubble of a module.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/eMrQj5Na51Ooxm1c20rz4-20251121-101356.png "Document image")

﻿

Next to the scenario name, click **Canvas options** and toggle the credit usage view on and off.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Jtobb7hQAFIAeayPY2hGd-20250912-121538.gif "Document image")

﻿

## Scenario history

﻿Scenario﻿ history tracks credits used per module run. To find it:

1. Go to **Scenarios** in the left sidebar.

2. Open a scenario﻿ in the scenario list.

3. Click the **History** tab at the top.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/rEpDxHpgyk-bKHVEFIDQm-20251121-100431.png "Document image")

﻿

Click **Details** to view a specific run in more detail.
