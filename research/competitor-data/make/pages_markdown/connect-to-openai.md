# Connect to OpenAI

1 min

In this guide, learn how to connect your MCP toolbox to OpenAI.

**Prerequisites**

* OpenAI Platform account

* ﻿[MCP toolbox URL and key](/mcp-toolboxes#create-an-mcp-toolbox)﻿

To connect your MCP toolbox to OpenAI:

1

In OpenAI's Playground, sign in to your OpenAI account.

2

Navigate to **Prompts**.

3

In **Tools**, click the + button.

4

Select **MCP Server**.

5

Click **Add new** to open a form.

![Document image](https://developers.make.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3035801395-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FyHSLDvK9bMDQI1lCXXt6%252Fuploads%252FmBUZGbPtiMSyuIO8X13z%252Fopenai%2520mcp%2520server.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dcda2743b-a3d7-4cf0-9171-b331c049878f&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=49860524&sv=2 "Document image")

﻿

6

In **URL**, add your MCP toolbox URL.

7

Name your MCP toolbox.

8

In **Authentication**, select **Access token / API key**.

9

Add your MCP toolbox key to the field that requires an access token.

10

Click **Connect**.

You have now connected your MCP toolbox server to OpenAI.

**MCP toolbox timeout:** Your MCP toolbox scenarios must finish running within 40 seconds. If a scenario runs longer, the connection times out and no outputs are returned to the client.
