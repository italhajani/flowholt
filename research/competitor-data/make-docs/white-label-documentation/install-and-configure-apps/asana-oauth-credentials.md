# Asana OAuth credentials

To configure Asana on your instance, you need to create OAuth 2.0 credentials in the Asana developers portal arrow-up-right and enter them in the Native Apps page of your instance. This OAuth 2.0 connection lets Asana share data with the modules in scenarios.

To avoid a known issue , follow steps 6 and 7 of the page Steps in Asana .

### hashtag Prerequisites

- An Asana developers account arrow-up-right
- Access to the Administration UI of your Make instance
