# Manage your email preferences

2 min

You can manage your email notification preferences from your **Profile** page. The options on the **Email preferences** tab let you customize which email notifications you receive from Make﻿ . You can opt-in to emails with information about specific topics and decide which scenario﻿-related notifications you receive from each organization you are a member of.

### Global notifications

The Global notifications section includes options for communication about Make﻿ overall. For example, you can choose to receive emails about updates to Make﻿, special offers and promotions, or announcements about the Make﻿ Academy.

### Organizations

﻿Make﻿ can automatically send notification emails when your scenario﻿ encounters a warning, an error, or a deactivation due to errors. By default, you receive emails in the following cases:

* An error prevents your scenario﻿ from successfully running.

* ﻿Make﻿ automatically disables your scenario﻿ due to errors.

You can customize your email notifications for each organization you belong to.

* **Deactivation** - these notifications inform you whenever Make﻿ disables a scenario﻿ because of errors.

* **Warning** - these notifications inform you of issues in a scenario﻿ that require your attention. For example, issues with an app's connections.

* **Errors** - these notifications inform you of issues that prevent a scenario﻿ from successfully completing.

﻿Make﻿ sends an email immediately when there is a warning or error. Then, Make﻿ watches for further warnings or errors and sends you a digest email (after 15 minutes for warnings and after 5 minutes for errors). This second email links to the scenario﻿ history where you can find more details.
