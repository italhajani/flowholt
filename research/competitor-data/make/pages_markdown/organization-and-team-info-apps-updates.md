# Organization and team info, apps updates

4 min

## UI update: Organization and team info visible again

You can now quickly check which **organization** and **team** you’re in while working on the Scenario Builder. When the [enhanced navigation](https://help.make.com/new-navigation-now-live-for-all-users "enhanced navigation") was introduced, this information was temporarily hidden. Simply hover over the scenario﻿ name to see your organization and team names again. This helps you confirm that you’re editing the correct scenario﻿ in the right workspace.
