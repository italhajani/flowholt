1. Debug your app

# Overview

Learn how to use the Make DevTool to debug your app.

During the development of your app, you may experience issues with your app and/or API. Therefore, you will need to use the Make DevTool or the console to debug your app.

Here are some examples of specific debugging issues you may encounter:

- Pagination in list and search modules
- Remote Procedure Calls (RPCs)
- Custom IML functions
