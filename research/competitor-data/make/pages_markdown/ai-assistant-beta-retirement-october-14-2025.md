# AI Assistant Beta retirement – October 14, 2025

0 min

The current Make AI Assistant will be retiring on **October 14, 2025** to make way for a smarter, next-generation AI experience.

**What this means:**

* AI Assistant Beta will no longer be available after **October 14, 2025**.

* When you open AI Assistant, a retirement orange banner will appear at the top of the AI Assistant panel.
