# Step 7. Add another module

1 min

Now that your Google Sheets trigger is working correctly, you need to add a second module to send notifications and connect it to your Slack workspace.

To add another module to your scenario:

1

Click the **+** icon on the right side of the Google Sheets module.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/zIGDHgzVzTymU72CcVR9R-20251103-195256.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

Alternatively, to add a new module, you can double-click anywhere in your canvas or right-click anywhere and select **Add a module** from the menu.

2

Search for **Slack** in the search bar or browse the available apps.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/aMCnU9QU9aCpjcRQniWyk-20251103-195326.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

3

Select **Slack** and then select **Send a** **Message** from the available modules.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/r_S_XclC0l5qxctDNFq4z-20251103-195351.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

4

After you add the module to your scenario, the connection settings will appear. Click **Create a connection.**

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/mfHPBOakWyz40inIpCZ_Q-20251103-195415.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

5

For **Connection type**, select **Slack (user)**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/a2ToVSTr8LRlZEfh-s2zF-20251103-195436.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

6

Name your connection, or leave the default name, and click **Save**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Qb0NuNeGbfhe8WVMCpG1D-20251103-195544.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

7

A Slack sign-in window will open. Click **Allow** to authorize Make to access your Slack account.

![Document image](https://app.archbee.com/api/optimize/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Er2QOciF6baGR7P9SLtwy-20250930-083722.png "Document image")

﻿

If you have multiple Slack workspaces, make sure to select the one where you created the "sales-team" channel.

With your Slack module added to the scenario, you're now ready to configure it to send notifications using data from your Google Sheets trigger. In the next step, you'll map data between these modules.
