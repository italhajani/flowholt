# Search scenario executions

1. Go to Administration > Recent executions .
2. Click the filter image next to Started or Status and enter or select your search criteria. Started You can define the time/date Since and/or Until to narrow your results. Status lets you search by scenario execution status: All Success Warning Error
3. Hit Enter .
