# What is Make﻿ Grid?

﻿Make﻿ Grid is a dependency map that helps businesses visually manage and optimize their entire automation and AI ecosystem. Think of it like a simulation of your automation infrastructure, it shows a layout of how everything is built and wired. Make﻿ Grid creates a near real-time, auto-generated map that shows how your scenarios, apps, data stores, and AI-powered components are connected, making it easier to debug, scale, and optimize your workflows.

# Key features of Make﻿ Grid

**Real-time visibility**:

﻿Make﻿ Grid updates automatically as changes occur, giving you a dynamic view of your system.

**Faster troubleshooting**:

﻿Make﻿ Grid shows indicators for errors in your scenarios, making it easier to troubleshoot, especially in multi-scenario workflows.

**Smarter scaling**:

﻿Make﻿ Grid helps trace data flows and identify inter-scenario dependencies and impacts before making changes. When restructuring folders, onboarding new users, or expanding your automation environment, Make﻿ Grid ensures all stakeholders have access to a consistent view of your automation architecture.

**Easy navigation**:

With the pan feature in Make﻿ Grid, you can move across different parts of the grid. It allows you to efficiently explore and interact with complex automation structures, especially when working with large teams or multiple folders. Right-click anywhere in the background and drag to pan the canvas or slide two fingers on the trackpad to pan around.

**Improved collaboration**:

You can work together with your team and see active teammates and their cursors in real-time. This shared visibility streamlines collaboration, supports faster and more accurate decision-making, and enables scalable growth.

**Improved resource tracking:**

With Make﻿ Grid, you can gain transparency into data flows, AI usage, or operations consumption.

To learn more about Make﻿ Grid, click the **Resources** icon in the top right corner of the page.
