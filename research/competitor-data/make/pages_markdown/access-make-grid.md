# Access Make Grid

3 min

Make Grid is available through the following three access points:

### Access Make﻿ Grid from the Make platform

1

Log in to your Make﻿ account.

2

In the left sidebar, click **Grid**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/eU7_7at7ve4Em19McQq8O-20251214-155506.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

Make Grid opens without focusing on any specific scenario.

### Access Make﻿ Grid from the scenario list

1

In the left sidebar, click **More** > **Scenarios**.

2

In the **All scenarios** list, click **Grid.**

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/vKIiGukoXinLcm8al5iD1-20251214-155918.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

Make Grid opens, focusing on the selected scenario.

### Access Make﻿ Grid from the scenario editor

1

In the **All scenarios** list, click on any of the scenarios.

2

In the top right corner, click **Open in** **Grid**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/xBPBRm43nOFcDX2eW1R9K-20260317-092250.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

Make Grid opens, focusing on the selected scenario.
