# What to do if your payment fails

1 min

If Make﻿ is unable to process your payment, you will receive an email. There is a seven-day grace period to [update your payment method](/change-payment-details)﻿. When the grace period ends, your subscription is automatically canceled.

If you use ACH direct debit for a failed payment, your subscription is renewed, but may be canceled if the new ACH payment fails.

If your payment fails, follow these steps:

1

In the left sidebar, click **Org**.

2

Under **My Plan**, click **Payments**.

3

Click **Finish payment**.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/oAyFj2GHlBeBVWF5OAir2/Y6sU1e7qVpa8XvypyxTcJ-20251007-115133.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

4

In the new tab, follow the on-screen instructions to enter a new payment method. You can enter either credit card or direct debit payment information.

5

Review the information you entered. Click **Pay** to begin the transaction.

6

Confirm the payment by checking the **Payments** tab.

ACH direct debit payments take up to five business days to complete. As a result, the **Payments** tab may not reflect a recent direct debit payment.
