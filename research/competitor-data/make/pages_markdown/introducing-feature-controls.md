# Introducing Feature controls

2 min

We've launched the Feature controls page, which provides simple **ON/OFF** toggles to independently enable or disable specific AI and beta capabilities across your organization.

This page controls only AI and beta capabilities, not entire application modules.

### **Key benefits**

**Strategic rollout**: You can now maintain complete control over the adoption rate of features. Easily enable or disable AI and beta capabilities at the organizational level to manage feature rollout.

**Risk management**: You caneffectively manage changes and ensure new features are introduced in a controlled and tested manner. This also ensures that maintain allignment with your internal governance policies.

**Manage specific capabilities**: With the Feature controls page, you can instantly manage access to key features, including:

* ﻿[Make AI Toolkit](https://apps.make.com/ai-tools "Make AI Toolkit")﻿

* ﻿[Make AI Agents](https://help.make.com/introduction-to-ai-agents "Make AI Agents")

### **Who can use it?**

This feature is available for all users on the **Enterprise plan**.

Only users with the Admin or Organization Owner role will be able to see and use the new feature controls. You can find it under **Organization** > **Feature controls.**
