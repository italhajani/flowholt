# User management improvements, new Google app

2 min

## Improvements and changes

* We’ve added a small but important improvement to the **Users** tab. Now you can filter users by their role at both organization and team levels. Simply click on the filter icon and select the role that you would like to see in the list.

![Document image](https://images.archbee.com/4CkrlJIBl1di_p1x71ery-t_5hA8ql6vmxwB80jL7Lg-20250228-114744.png?format=webp "Document image")

﻿

## Fixed issues

* Recently, when searching for modules in the scenario﻿ editor, the **Instant** tag was missing from instant triggers and they were mistakenly labeled as **ACID** (polling) triggers. This issue has been resolved, and it now works as expected.

## Apps updates

### New apps:

* ﻿[Google Search Console](https://www.make.com/en/help/apps/marketing/google-search-console "Google Search Console") - This app provides additional online marketing support to monitor and troubleshoot your website’s Google Search results.

* ﻿[Flodesk](https://www.make.com/en/help/apps/marketing/flodesk "Flodesk") - This app allows users to create visually engaging emails for designing and managing email marketing campaigns.
